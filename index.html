Canada's Carbon Emissions (1990-2018)

Canada is one of the world's largest nations... not just by land mass!

We are the world's 10th largest economy (out of 205) (World Bank, 2020a).

We are also the world's 10th largest emitter of greenhouse gasses (GHGs) (Climate Watch, 2021).

We make quite the impact for the world's 39th largest country by population at about 38 million Canadians (World Bank, 2020b).

Canadian Climate Policy Impacts (at 2030)

Canada has made a few promises on climate change (Office of the Auditor General of Canada, n.d.).

Our current goals were made in 2015 (Office of the Auditor General of Canada, n.d.) and have expanded in 2020
(Environment and Climate Change Canada, 2021).

Overall, Canada wants to reduce emissions to 30% below 2005 levels by 2030
and eventually reach net-zero emissions by 2050
